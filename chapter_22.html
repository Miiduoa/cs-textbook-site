Chapter 22 — Social and Ethical Issues

← Chapter 21 Appendix A →

Privacy

Personal data collection raises concerns about consent, surveillance, and misuse. Strong privacy policies and minimal data retention help reduce risk.

Bias & Fairness

Algorithms trained on biased data can produce unfair outcomes. Auditing, diverse data, and transparency are critical.

Intellectual Property

Copyright, patents, and licenses define how software and media can be used or shared.

Environmental Impact

Data centers consume significant energy. Efficient hardware and renewable energy reduce carbon footprint.
