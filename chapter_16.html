English Reading Guide (中文導讀)

這章英文核心是 lossless/lossy 與 entropy。讀到壓縮比時，先看「原始/壓縮」的分子分母。

  • Huffman = 頻率越高，碼越短。
  • LZW = 字典壓縮。
  • 看到 bitrate 就是「每秒位元數」。

Learning Objectives

  • Explain why compression is possible.
  • Compare lossless and lossy methods.
  • Compute compression ratio and savings.
  • Describe Huffman and LZW at a high level.

Key Terms

lossless, lossy, entropy, Huffman, LZW, bitrate

Worked Example: Compression Ratio

Original 100 MB → Compressed 25 MB ⇒ Ratio = 100 / 25 = 4×, Savings = 75%.

Compression Basics

Compression reduces storage or transmission cost by encoding data more efficiently.

  • Lossless: original data fully recoverable (ZIP, PNG).
  • Lossy: discards some detail (JPEG, MP3).

Common Methods

MethodTypeIdea
HuffmanLosslessShort codes for frequent symbols
LZWLosslessDictionary of repeated patterns
JPEGLossyFrequency transform + quantization
MP3LossyPsychoacoustic modeling
