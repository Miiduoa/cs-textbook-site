IEEE 754 Floating‑Point

Floating‑point numbers use three fields: sign, exponent, mantissa.

FormatSignExponentMantissa
Single (32‑bit)1823
Double (64‑bit)11152

Floating‑Point Addition

Steps:

  1. Align exponents (shift smaller mantissa).
  2. Add mantissas.
  3. Normalize result.
  4. Round and check for overflow/underflow.

Interactive: Float Add (Simplified)

Result: 6.25

Precision & Rounding

Limited mantissa bits cause rounding errors. Example: 0.1 + 0.2 ≠ 0.3 exactly in binary floating‑point.
