Appendix D — Structure Chart

← Appendix C Appendix E →

Overview

A structure chart shows the hierarchical organization of modules and the control/data flow between them.

Coupling & Cohesion

Good design aims for high cohesion within modules and low coupling between modules.

Interactive: Fan‑in / Fan‑out

Fan‑in/Fan‑out ratio: 0.60

Practice Set

  1. Why is high fan‑out risky?
  2. Give an example of low‑cohesion module.
  3. Describe how a structure chart helps maintenance.
