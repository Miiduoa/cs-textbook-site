What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) aims to build systems that perform tasks requiring human intelligence: perception, reasoning, learning, and decision‑making.

Machine Learning Types

TypeGoalExample
SupervisedLearn from labeled dataSpam detection
UnsupervisedFind structureClustering
ReinforcementLearn by rewardGame playing
